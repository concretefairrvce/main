CONferral

Event description:

Congratulations! It’s that time of the year where your work might just be the “BEST PROJECT” that anyone has ever seen. We bring you the CONferral competition: a technical paper presentation competition that can be the right answer to your ever-growing curiosity. Your technical paper should be a novel drive either experimental or hypothetical that sheds light on the core parts of our area. This is the chance to exhibit your curiosity, advancement, and utilizations of advanced technologies. The event is an interdisciplinary event, with Civil Engineering applications.



Team Size:

Maximum of 4 participants in a team. .



Cash Prize:

₹3000 – Winners | ₹1500 – Runners up


Deadlines:

Abstract Submission: 4th December
Abstract Selection Results: 6th December
Full Paper Submission: 10th December
Paper Selection Results: 11th December
Technical paper Presentation: 15th December




Rules and regulations:

  • The abstract should have not more than 300 words. It must be mailed to the concretefairrvce@gmail.com by within deadline. The subject of the Mail should be formatted to (Abstract: college name : Team ID)
  • The selected teams, must submit the full paper must be as per ASCE format. It must to mail to the concretefairrvce@gmail.com within deadline. The subject of the mail should be formatted to
    (FPS : College name : Team ID) (Mention Link / Attach the copy).
  • Selected Papers will be intimated for the Technical Paper Presentation. Invite link for the session will be mailed to the corresponding author.
  • Each team will be given stipulated time for presentation, which will be followed by a Q&A session by the Judging Panel and fellow participants.
  • Winning Papers will be sent for peer review to biannual RVCE Journal, an ISSN publication of the university.
