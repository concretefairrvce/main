Concrete Fair

The Department of Civil Engineering, R V College of Engineering had been organizing the Concrete Fair successfully for the last 9 years. It has evolved with every edition to what it is today. The Concrete Fair is the largest technical fest that sees the participation of students from colleges all across the nation. The highly competent events and the sense of festivity that surrounds the fest is the best experience for all those who witness it. Over the years the Concrete Fair has become an integral part of the departmental activities and we look forward to hosting this event every year. Owning to the current Covid-19 pandemic, we thus choose to continue the tradition through the online platform. The organizing committee is working towards conducting an equally cherishable edition of the fest. We wish to see your active participation and hope to make Concrete fair 2021 a successful event. The events have been carefully planned to ensure the registrants can participate from the safety of their homes.

CON.dwg

In this day and age, we truly believe in the power of a drawing to communicate and represent our imaginations more than what you see on the surface and we are excited to present one such platform to showcase your skills as the future Civil Engineers. “CON.dwg” is an event where participants will showcase their AUTOCAD, design and planning skills. This is a single round event which consists of design, detailing and planning of a Given topic, all of which has to be done on AutoCAD with proper Specifications.

CONferral

Congratulations! It’s that time of the year where your work might just be the best project that anyone has ever seen. We bring you the “CONferral” competition: a technical paper presentation competition that can be the right answer to your ever-growing curiosity. Your technical paper should be a novel drive either experimental or hypothetical that sheds light on the core parts of our area. This is the chance to exhibit your curiosity, advancement, and utilizations of advanced technologies. The event is an interdisciplinary event, with Civil Engineering applications.

CONio.h

Gone are those days where manual efforts were required to solve any given problem. Gather your friends as we bring to you all new competition “CONio.h”, which is an interdisciplinary coding competition aimed at solving Civil Engineering Problems with the help of Programming and Coding. Let’s show the world the power of collaboration as this event along with many other events this year aims to bring together students from different branches of Engineering to work together, learn, promote and prepare the “Interdisciplinary generation” of Science and Engineering.

CONnectivity

Who doesn’t like to travel? It is travelling that exposes us to various cultures and ethnicities. But is travelling through conventional methods sustainable? To answer this question, we bring to you the “CONectivity” event: an interdisciplinary event where the participants have to find the best path/way/route between two given locations around the world (Ex: UB city to Dublin) and submit the report within 6 hours.

CONquest

All the quizzers out there, Let’s see if you can answer this question. Which is the best way to learn about Civil Engineering while standing a chance to win in a national level competition? Yes, you got it right, it is the “CONquest” competition: a quiz-based event that will include multiple rounds to test the knowledge of the participants in the field of Civil Engineering. The limited time frame to outperform others is what will bring out the best in you. The rounds in this event may consist of multiple-choice questions, picture connects, crossword puzzles etc.

CONserve

This competition aims to bring out research and development acumen to student, where students can submit a paper (after conduction of experiment) or a research paper or model and ideas prior to the date of the event and have to present on the day of the event based on utilization of waste in concrete, mortar and also low-cost building materials in order to reduce both cost and environmental impacts.


CONsign

As the famous saying goes, “Creativity is intelligence having fun”, here is your chance to explore your creativity. We bring to you "CONsign" , which is a poster-making competition designed to feed that curious and creative brain of yours. Each participant has to come up with his/her ideas on the given problem statement and make a poster for the same using any software.!

CONstorm

“Everything begins with an idea”, Who is excited to see new ideas that make life simpler? We definitely are! Presenting you, the much awaited “CONstorming” event: A Start-up event filled with enthusiasm and the adrenaline rush to come up with new ideas that stimulate the innovator inside us. This interdisciplinary event will consist of 3 rounds. The start-up idea can be from any aspect of Engineering but must include aspects of Civil Engineering.

CONsculpt

Are you ready to turn imaginations into reality? if yes, then this event is for you. “CONsculpt” is an event which is designed to showcase your drafting and modelling skills. You get to prepare plans, elevations and 3D renderings of a building according to the stated requirements. You need to then present your concept of the building through video renderings of your 3D model. So, go to your drawing boards and start conceptualizing your design!

CONtender

India holds a heritage of magnificent buildings lasting through decades! CONtender is an event based on Estimation, Costing and valuation. It comprises 2 rounds of competition to test the estimation skills as well as tender skills. The primary motive of this event is to optimize the material usage to get the most effective estimate for the construction of the plan provided, without compromising the serviceability of the building and finally submitting a formal tender request. Do you have what it takes to get your tender request approved? We think you do!
